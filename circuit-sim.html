⚡ VoltSPICE Help Center

Welcome to the ultimate CAD-grade simulation engine! Here's how to build your circuits:

  • Dragging & Adding: Tap sidebar items to spawn components. Touch/click a component and move your finger/mouse to drag it around smoothly.
  • Step-by-Step Wiring: Turn on Wire Mode in the top bar. Tap the starting terminal pin of a component, then tap the destination pin of another component to connect them orthogonally.
  • Interactions: Double-click/Tap any Input Switch to toggle its binary state. Selecting any component highlights its net and reveals property editors.
  • Rotations & Shortcuts: Tap any component and press R to rotate it 90 degrees! Use Delete / Backspace to remove nodes or selected wires, Ctrl+D to duplicate a component, and standard Ctrl+Z / Ctrl+Y to Undo/Redo actions.
  • Pinch-To-Zoom & Pan: Scroll the mouse wheel or pinch-to-zoom on touchscreens to zoom into the mouse pointer. Drag the canvas using middle or right-click to pan freely.
  • Wire Deletion: Click or tap the midpoint bubble of any connection wire to select it, then tap the Delete button to remove it.
  • Fit View: Click the 🔲 Fit View button or press F key to automatically fit all nodes perfectly centered on screen!
