About Me

Hello! I'm Anuj, an electronics undergraduate from Delhi who enjoys making hardware come alive with code. My journey started with the classic blinking-LED on an Arduino, and quickly grew into building complete embedded systems — password-protected doors, gas-leak alarms, and automated fan controllers.

What makes my workflow a little different: I've learned to build and experiment from anywhere — writing Python in Pydroid 3, running Linux tools in Termux, simulating circuits in Proteus and Wokwi, and training in Google Colab. No excuses, just building.

Alongside electronics, I'm strengthening my computer-science fundamentals — Data Structures & Algorithms, DBMS, and Operating Systems — because great embedded engineers understand both the silicon and the software.
